Daniel George Wilson

Welcome to my official web page explaining who I am and the many projects I have made throughout the years of education and career in my life.

Information Communication Technology

My main interest in a career is working with Information Communication Technology (ICT) as a graphic designer and animator. This is where I am good at designing new graphics and supporting the world of animation.

Performing Arts

Outside the world of ICT, I also have a second interest, which is acting and performing in front of people. When I left school I completed a 2 year Level 3 Btec Performing Arts course at Lichfield College, which involved me being part of a bunch of shows at the Lichfield Garrick Theatre.
