Portfolio

Here are a few samples of the work I produced in my ICT and Performing Arts college courses.

Cyclemon Website

Here I made a website based on a cycle route event in the theme of the Japanese cartoon and gaming series Pokemon.

Toy Story The 4th Promo

This is where I made an animated movie trailer using Google Web Desinger to promote a new upcoming Disney Pixar film (unoffical).

The Blazing Of The Teletubbies Trailer

Where I made a movie trailer to complete the scenario of making a linear multimedia product.

All Shook Up

One of the shows I was part of at the Lichfield Garrick Theatre in the first year of my Level 3 Btec performing arts course at Lichfield College.
